Timeline of Ayush Hardeniya

July 2006 – 2011

Birth & Roots

Where it all began.

Born in a rural heartland, where simplicity sowed the seeds of strength.

A childhood surrounded by soil, fields, and the essence of joint family bonding. This was more than birth — this was grounding. Running through farms, playing in the mud, and learning the earliest values from grandparents. This phase laid the emotional and ethical foundation of my life.

2011 – 2015

The River & the Routines

The era of simplicity and smiles.

The mornings on river bridges with Grandpa. Life was raw and rooted.

Waking up at 4 AM and heading to sugarcane fields, walking along the river bridge with Grandpa — this wasn’t just routine, it was legacy transfer. Festivals felt like family reunions. Living village life in its purest form, this era gifted me memories that no city ever could.

2015 – 2019

CBS - The Real School of Life

The learning playground.

Creativity bloomed, individuality took shape, and curiosity became the habit.

At CBS Public School, I didn’t just study — I discovered. From building desktop models out of thermocol to performing dances, speeches, and school plays, every project shaped a layer of who I am.

Projects done:

  • Thermocol Desktop System
  • Roman Numerals 1–100 with matchsticks (15-day challenge)
  • Acting in plays, dance fests, and speech deliveries
  • Participated in IISF Delhi visit
This era was the incubation of self-expression, confidence, and curiosity.

2019 – 2023

The Rise Through Boards & Beyond

Trials, Triumphs & Transformation.

Losing Grandpa, scoring 96%, and biking 70 kms/day — that’s the grit.

Board exams came and I hit a 96+% in class 10 (passed in 2021), a moment of pride for the family. But 2021 also took away my Grandpa in the last quarter — one of the heaviest emotional hits of my life. Despite this, I continued school, driving 70 km a day with my brother for Class 11–12, and finally passed out in 2023.

This phase brought with it golden friendships, little heartbreaks, and all the realism school books never mentioned. It was life’s true curriculum.

2023 – Present

The Execution Era

From Learning to Doing.

Still in uniform, but now crafting identity.

This isn’t just a college — it’s a launchpad. I entered this phase with a mindset to act, not just absorb. From leading projects, working on my personal brand, starting my fitness transformation, handling both tech and temple at dawn — this is the real Ayush in motion.

The journey now is not just academic, it’s personal, professional, and purpose-driven. This is the version that gets better every version.
